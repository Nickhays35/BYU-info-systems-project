This is the Ballot Changes Page

There is a couple of aspects that affect the names that appear on the Ballot every year

  1. Someone got inducted
      2. If someone was inducted when they appeared on the Ballot the previous year, they will not appear on the ballot for this year
  2. If someone ran out of years of eligibility or otherwise was ommited for failure to recieve votes
    • Each player gets 10 years of being on the ballot if they meet the requirements to appear. They must be retired from play for 5 years proceeding their appearance on the ballot
    • If a player fails to recieve at least 5% of the previous years vote they are removed from the ballot even if they have at least 10 years of eligibility
  3. New players are added to the Ballot
    • Each year, new players whom have just reached the mark of five years retired from play are added to the Ballot
